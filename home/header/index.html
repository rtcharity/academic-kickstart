About

Rethink Priorities is a project of Rethink Charity. Right now, our research agenda is focused on trying to learn how to apply cost-effectiveness frameworks to uncertain domains, prioritization and research work within interventions aimed at nonhuman animals, and assisting LEAN and SHIC in gathering evidence about EA movement building.
